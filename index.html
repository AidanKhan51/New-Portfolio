3D scene incorporating custom hard-surface models and online assets. Intended as practice for environment and lighting design, as well as hard-surface and asset development pipelines. Furthermore, practice in translating a 2D concept into 3D space using lighting, texturing, and scale.

This process involved assets and textures from free online stores to save time, but many models were either entirely made or heavily modified by myself. Beyond that, I placed a heavy emphasis on the age and wear in this scene. For example, the use of dust on the hardwood, or the filing cabinet drawers being opened and misaligned made the space feel used and alive.
